Rules:

  • The first player throws the dices and scores as many points as the total shown on the dices, making sure the dices donâ€™t roll a 1 (each or both).
  • The player may continue rolling and accumulating points (but risks rolling a 1) or ends his turn.
  • If the player rolls a 1, his turn is over, he loses all the points he has accumulated during that turn, and he passes the dices to the next player.
  • The winner is the first to achieve the final score. If the final score is empty, the default score will be 100 points.

Good luck!
